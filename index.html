Our Day

The Story of the Day

A guide to what's happening when!

2:15 pm

Ceremony

Meet us at Pyrmont for the ceremony! The Registry asks that all guests arrive at the venue with plenty of time before the ceremony itself, so please arrive at 2:15 for a 2:30 start!

3:10 pm

Group Photos at Pyrmont

The ceremony will wrap up, and we'll head outside and grab some group photos in front of the Sydney skyline!

3:30 pm

Break

Susan and George will head off for family and couple photos, so it's break time! Feel free to get some city time in, or feel welcome to head to the reception venue at the Winery in Surry Hills.

5:30 pm

Welcome Drinks

Join us in the the Winery to kick off the reception dinner with some welcome cocktails! Drinks will be open for wine, beer, and even the spirits menu.

6:30 pm

Dinner

A three course dinner will be served, with quick toasts, cake and more beverages to follow!
